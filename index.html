About Me

Yunjie Zhang is a first-year doctoral student at Harvard GSD, focusing on political-ecological aspects in the making of city-region, among other dimensions, in an overlapped research fields of environmental history, planning history and political ecology. In particular, she studies how formal state actors intervene in integrated ecosystems that straddle political borders, how such interventions interact with—or displace—local community governance, and how planning and design instruments are mobilized by different actors.
